SNU 0D Mode - The non-existant SNU mode

Welcome to SNU 4D mode

Notice: The human eye can not natively see in 4 dimensions. It is going to look like weird 3D for the inexperienced.

This mode of the site is under construction, it will mainly just be a copy of 3D mode, but with 4D graphics.

Will there ever be a 5D mode?

There likely won't be an official 5D mode unless it is highly requested enough, and enough people want it and are willing to work on a 5D mode.

We could go out as far as we want as you can just keep adding in more and more axis values, it will just begin to look strange.
